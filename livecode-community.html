Full Stack Web Development

LeWagon Bootcamp for Full Stack Web Development is a 10 week intensive coding course that teaches participants the basics of Web Development including conceptualization, design, front- and backend development. Having already had experience with frontend development and a basic knowledge of HTML & CSS, I decided it was time to take my coding skills to the next level and learn more about what happens in the background.

As the final project of the bootcampt my idea was to create a platform to fix the very problem that every person learning to code is confronted with on a daily basis. Who can I ask when I don't know the answer to a coding problem? The solution was Livecode Community, a platform enabling aspiring coders to post their specific coding problems and enter into a video chat with someone who knows the solution.

Our Product Development Journey

The Homepage

Upon registering as a user by signing up with your email and chosen password, the first thing you see is the ticket homepage showing you all the existing tickets from other users. Color coding indicates the status of every ticket - green indictating open tickets, orange for tickets in progress and white for tickets who have already been answered and are therefore closed. The search bar at the top makes it possible to search all existing tickets to check if your specific question might already have been answered.

Creating your own Request

If you had a go checking the existing requests but didn't find what you are looking for, then it's time to raise your own ticket and creating your own request. Upon doing that you have to give your request a title, a proper description and choose the languages that relate to your current problem. Once you've inputted all the necessary information it ist time to hit the Create Ticket button and wait for an expert to accept your request!

Video Call & Screen Sharing

Once someone accepted your ticket you will receive a notification in your messages tab and you can answer that person in the chat. Once you have established, that your expert is ready and has time to explain to you how to solve the problem, you can both hop on a video call and speak to each other live. Additionally you can also share your own screen with the other person to show your code and make sure they understand the details of your specific problem.

Profiles & Reviews

Once you've fixed the coding problem and finished the call, it is time to leave your evaluation for the call through a review form. Another feature of the platform is the possibility to look at other users profiles and see their past reviews to determine wether you would like to accept help from them or not. Profiles include a name, short description, a changeable profile picture and reviews from past tickets in a five-star rating format.
